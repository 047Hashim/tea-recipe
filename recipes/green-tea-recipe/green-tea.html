Green Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of green tea leaves or 1 green tea bag
  • 1 teaspoon of honey (optional)
  • 1 slice of lemon (optional)

Steps to Make Green Tea

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan or kettle.
  2. Let the water cool slightly for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of green tea leaves or a green tea bag.
  4. Steep for 2-3 minutes (do not over-steep to avoid bitterness).
  5. Optional: Add honey or lemon for flavor.
  6. Stir well, strain if using loose leaves, and enjoy!
