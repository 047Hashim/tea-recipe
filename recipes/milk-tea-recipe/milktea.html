Milk Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of loose tea leaves or a tea bag
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar (optional)

Steps to Make Milk Tea

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves or a tea bag.
  3. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in 1/2 cup of milk and bring to a boil.
  5. Stir in sugar to taste and mix well.
  6. Strain the tea into a cup and enjoy!
